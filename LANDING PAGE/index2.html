ACCREDITATION PARTNERS

Continuing Professional Development (CPD)

Continuing Professional Development (CPD) is the proactive commitment to ongoing lifelong learning. It is the process by which professionals track, document, and enhance their knowledge, skills, and experience throughout their careers—beyond their initial formal qualifications.Continuing Professional Development represents the intentional maintenance and enhancement of the knowledge and skills required to deliver a professional service of the highest quality throughout a career.

The Association of Healthcare Trainers (AoHT)

The AoHT is one of the UK’s largest non-profit membership associations for professionals who deliver healthcare training. Its aiming to elevate the standards of training delivery to ensure that healthcare professionals are equipped with the most current, evidence-based knowledge and skills. By fostering a community of practice, the AoHT provides a unified voice for trainers, advocating for the importance of high-quality education as a fundamental component of patient safety and effective care.

Skill Development Council (SDC)

The Skill Development Council Canada (SDC Canada) provides international accreditation, and certification services to training institutions around the world. This process of accreditation is not merely a badge of honor but a comprehensive evaluative journey that ensures an institution possesses the necessary infrastructure, qualified faculty, and robust assessment protocols to produce competent graduates. Through these services, SDC Canada helps bridge the gap between local training outputs and the demands of the global labor market, ensuring that skills acquired in one region are respected and understood in another.

Federal Ministry of Education

The Federal Ministry of Labour and Employment supports labour administration and policy, promoting employment and productive engagement between employers and workers.
